### 1. **Service Worker**

## ðŸŽ¨ Customization

### App Icons
Replace the placeholder icons with your own:
- `public/icon-192x192.svg` (192x192px)
- `public/icon-512x512.svg` (512x512px)

Preferred formats:
- PNG or SVG
- Square aspect ratio
- Support for transparency

### App Colors
Edit `vite.config.ts` and `index.html`:
- Change `theme_color` to your brand color
- Change `background_color` for the splash screen

### App Metadata
Edit `public/manifest.json`:
- Update `name`, `short_name`, and `description`
- Modify `categories`
- Add screenshots for app stores
